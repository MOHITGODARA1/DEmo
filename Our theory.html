About Us

Meghavi Wellness Spa is a calming haven for the busy soul that is cluttered with the chaos popularised by the hustle culture. Since our
inception in 2014, the spa has emerged as a wellness brand that strongly believes in providing quality treatments by highly trained and
well-experienced therapists.

The name ‘Meghavi’ is an extension of our co-founder’s name – Megha Dinesh. Besides the medical field, the spa industry is the only other profession that involves touching the body. While there are medicines to relieve muscular pain, therapeutic touch is a proven way of relieving pain and stress in the body. Even then, somehow, the spa industry in India does not get its due recognition. The idea of extending her name to the brand is our co-founder’s attempt to give a face and identity to more than 7 million people in India who are involved in this profession and thereby establish a connection with our guests.

We provide a holistic experience to our guests that helps rejuvenate and promote better health. Our therapies are curated keeping in mind our core belief that wellness massages are not a rare luxury treatment but a periodic necessity for a healthy mind & body.

We are proud to have partnered with esteemed hospitality brands like Novotel Hotels & Resorts, Marriott Hotels & Resorts, Hilton, Emaar, Radisson, Intercontinental Hotels Group, The Fern Hotels & Resorts and more. Moreover, you will also find Meghavi Wellness Spa centres at various airports in India so you can enjoy our therapies as you wait to board for your next escape.
